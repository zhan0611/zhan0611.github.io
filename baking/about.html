Sweet Bakery is handmade using fresh, natural ingredients. Why are sweet bread and desserts so popular? Because we retain the bread baking method that has been passed down from 1889. The best bread comes from the best flour, and the best flour comes from Camp Remy wheat. Sweet Bakery uses only the highest quality natural ingredients and contains no additives or preservatives. Sweet Bakery uses flour from more than 8,650 acres of wheat grown by 300 Canadian farmers. The flour used by the sweet bakery has maintained close ties with these farmers and strictly controlled the quality of wheat production. Sweet bakery uses wheat that can be milled and stored only in 7 specific mills. Allow milled flour to be stored for three weeks before using it. Sweet bakery takes about 7 hours to make bread, and works 24 hours at any time, so we can provide customers with fresh handmade bread.
